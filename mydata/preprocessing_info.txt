The data in this directory is the result of the preprocessing and cleaning steps performed on the original data from the `../data` directory.

- The `1/` subdirectory contains the initial cleaned versions of the datasets, generated by `1_clean_data.ipynb` and `1_clean_airports.ipynb`.
- The `2/` subdirectory contains a second version of the cleaned datasets with additional features extracted from the raw JSON files, generated by `2_clean_data.ipynb`.
